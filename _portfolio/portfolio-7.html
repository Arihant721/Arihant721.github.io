Objective:

BITS Pilani, a notable university in India where I did my undergrad, has a team that competes in SAE BAJA competitions. Given that we were trying to improve our BAJA ATV for AWD drive trains, a new gearbox had to be designed.

Methodology

  • Conceptualised a 4-gear reduction gearbox with 1 bevel, to balance force output to both front and back tyres in a 40% - 60% ratio.
  • Created an initial concept gearbox and revised the design to a more compact version with the help of other teammates.
  • Using gear-ratio, force and torque calculations done by another teammate, FEA simulations were performed to ascertain stresses and FoS, to improve design of the gears and the box.

Results

  • Forces and stresses were finally obtained to be within limits, while adhering to other design constraints and the SAE rulebook.
  • While this could not be manufactured when I was there, the junior team members were able to manufacture a prototype based on our design.
