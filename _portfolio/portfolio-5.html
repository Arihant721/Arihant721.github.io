Objective:

For a prototype heat exchanger developed by a PhD student at BITS Pilani, sensors and instrumentation were required to be setup, as well as some assembly.

Methodology

  • Setup pressure, humidity and thermal sensors into specified locations of the test rig.
  • Helped construct and assemble some components inside the test rig, including coating the heat exchanger coils with the desiccant.
  • Utilised LabVIEW to collect data from the sensors and calibrate them, based on instructions provided by the manufacturer.
  • Ran multiple tests to troubleshoot, verify sensors and repair.
  • Connected the logged data feeds from the sensors to a control sequence in LabVIEW to actuate values and shutters, as needed to alternate flows during the cycle.

Results

  • Final test run was proper and gave good results.
