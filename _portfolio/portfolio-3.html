Objective:

My team at ASM's America office tasked me with suggesting design improvements to the gas delivery systems of its process machines for a wider range of operating conditions.

Methodology

  • Representative images below so as to not disclose details.
  • Identified components in the gas delivery systems, that did not meet the wider operating conditions and searched for replacement alternatives.
  • Performed key calculations for replacements of some components.
  • Made CAD models on PTC Creo for these new components and investigated for proper spatial fit and connections available that satisfy leak-proof and corrosion requirements.
  • Clarified important doubts with vendors of these components, and documented everything using SAP ERP (later S/4 HANA), our PLM software.
  • Collaborated with product support and field service engineers for testing of the newer gas delivery systems and trouble-shooting.

Results

  • Was able to identify and document components for replacement.
  • Presented these results both to my team and to other employees in the final intern presentation.
